COMPUTER ENGINEERING

About the Department

The Department of Computer Engineering & Information Technology was established in the year 2001 and provides an outstanding academic environment complemented by excellence in teaching. The Department offers 4 years undergraduate course (B.E.) and 2 years graduate course (M.E.). Course offered under Department of CE & IT

The Department has state-of-the-art infrastructure and computing facilities bearing Gujarat Technological University (GTU) curriculum. Following laboratories are available for different fields of computer and IT.

  • Operating System Lab
  • Java Lab
  • Internet Technology Lab
  • Networking Lab
  • System Software Lab
  • Open source software Lab

Educational Objectives of the Department

Computer industries require a large number of experts with different skills. The department has strong collaboration with industries and has signed MOU with many leading companies.
